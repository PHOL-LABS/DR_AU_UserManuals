\chapter{Introduction}\label{ch:introduction}

This operating manual covers the \DRAudi{} digital instrument cluster for Audi Coupe, Coupe Quattro, Quattro/Sport, 80~(B2), 90, and 4000 vehicles. It summarises the hardware variants, describes their functions, and explains how to install, configure, operate, store, and maintain the dashboard. The guidance is intended for vehicle owners, automotive electricians, and service centres that retrofit the product.

The chapters that follow detail the model identification scheme, connector pin-outs, operating conditions, and installation procedures required to replace the factory instrumentation. Maintenance, configuration, and troubleshooting references ensure the instrument panel can be serviced without relying on the original Russian-language documentation.
