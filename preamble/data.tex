%% Abstract (recommended length around 80-200 words)
\newcommand*{\ManualAbstract}{%
    This user manual documents the \DRAudi{} digital instrument cluster designed for Audi Coupe, Coupe Quattro, Quattro/Sport, 80~(B2), 90, and 4000 vehicles.
    It explains how the dashboard replaces the factory panel, decodes the model coding scheme for the \DRAudiGreen{} and \DRAudiRed{} variants, and summarises the supplied hardware.
    The guide details the electrical interfaces, connector pin-outs, operating principle, and measurement capabilities, and provides the environmental limits required for reliable use.
    Step-by-step instructions cover removal of the factory cluster, repinning of Audi harnesses, day-to-day operation, and the multi-function (MFA) features.
    Dedicated chapters describe maintenance through the USBasp programmer, configuration via the Bluetooth terminal or miniUIOD Wi-Fi interface, and troubleshooting workflows for typical issues.
    Final sections summarise labelling, packaging, and storage recommendations and provide a change log for the manual.
}
